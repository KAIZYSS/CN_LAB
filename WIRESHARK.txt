🔥 STEP 1: OPEN WIRESHARK
Open Wireshark
Select your network:
Wi-Fi (usually)
Ethernet (if using LAN)

👉 Double click to start capture

🔥 STEP 2: GENERATE TRAFFIC (IMPORTANT)

👉 Open browser and visit:

YouTube
Google
Any website

👉 Keep browsing for ~2 minutes

🔥 STEP 3: STOP CAPTURE

👉 Click red square (stop button)

🔥 STEP 4: FILTER UDP PACKETS

In filter bar (top), type:

udp

👉 Press Enter

👉 Now only UDP packets will show ✔

🔥 STEP 5: OPEN IO GRAPH

Go to:

Statistics → IO Graph
🔥 STEP 6: CONFIGURE GRAPH

In IO Graph window:

Set:
Tick Interval → 1 sec
Graph 1 filter →
udp

👉 This shows UDP traffic over time

🔥 STEP 7: SET 2-MIN WINDOW

👉 If needed:

Drag timeline OR
Just show full capture (~2 mins)
🔥 STEP 8: TAKE OUTPUT

👉 Take screenshot of:

UDP packets (filtered view)
IO Graph
